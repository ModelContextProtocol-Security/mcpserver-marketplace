# Community Forums and Discussion Platforms

## Definition

Social platforms where users share MCP server recommendations and experiences. Trust is based on community reputation and social signals.

## Characteristics

- User experiences and recommendations
- Community vetting (upvotes, comments)
- Reputation-based trust
- No formal verification
- Malicious actors can participate

## Trust Model

- Community reputation and voting
- User verification of claims
- No central authority

## Security Opportunities

- Community can identify and warn about malicious servers
- User experiences provide real-world security information
- Fast propagation of security warnings

## Security Risks

- Astroturfing (fake positive reviews)
- Malicious actors can appear legitimate
- AI agents scraping may not evaluate discussion quality
- No removal mechanism for bad recommendations
- Social engineering possible

## Evaluation Priority

**Medium** - Indirect influence, users generally skeptical, AI agents scraping may trust inappropriately, fast-moving discussions may contain outdated info.
